There have been lots of stories this week – and thank you to everyone who’s forwarded them to me – about a pigeon found with a beaded vest.

“Is This Your Bedazzled Pigeon?” asks Gizmodo.

“Did you lose a pigeon dressed in a tiny bedazzled vest? He’s been found,” says Mashable.

As the story comes from Arizona, no surprise, AZCentral has coverage too: “Bird rescue searches for owner of rhinestone vest-wearing pigeon.”
